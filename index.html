The Concept

Structured Skill Development

Our Academy offers a structured training program designed for licensed pilots who want to refine their skills, gain confidence, or enjoy flying together with other pilots under the supervision of an instructor.

Expert Coaching

We provide expert guidance in thermalling, XC flying, advanced ground handling, safety maneuvers, and hike & fly techniques, combining practical training and in-depth theory to boost your decision-making and risk assessment.

Community & Equipment

Enjoy a supportive flying community, exclusive training days, and personalized equipment advice. Academy members receive exclusive discounts on tested, top-tier gear tailored to their skills and goals.
